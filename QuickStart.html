🚀 PitchCompare: Quick Start Guide

Follow these five steps to set up your own pairwise pitch evaluation session.

01
Create Your Account

Go to the Professor Portal and click Sign Up. Enter your email and use the Invite Code: JOIN-PITCH. This grants you access to create and manage your own secure Pitch Rooms.

02
Initialize a Pitch Room

In your My Rooms dashboard, click + New Pitch Room. Give it a recognizable name and a unique 6-character Room Code (e.g., DEMO12). Note: Rooms are private to your account; other faculty cannot access your room data.

03
Upload Your Data

Download the CSV templates from the "About" page. You will need to upload two files:

  • students.csv: Columns: email, name. (Only these users can vote).
  • pitches.csv: Columns: title, url, author_emails. (Prevents authors from voting for their own work).
⚠️ Important: All video URLs must be hosted on YouTube (we highly recommend instructing students to set their visibility to "Unlisted").
04
Launch the Session

Share the URL and your Room Code with your group. For optimal leaderboard stability, we recommend assigning 5 to 10 comparisons per participant, depending on the length of the pitch videos.

05
Export Results

Monitor progress in real-time. Once voting is complete, click Export Results to download a final CSV of the rankings and Elo scores.

The Logic: This system uses the Elo Rating System to capture collective intuition. It eliminates the "subjectivity trap" of traditional rubrics by focusing on simple, head-to-head comparisons.
